Injuries

This page is to provide information about different types of sports injuries, including

  • Ankle
  • Shin
  • Knee
  • Wrist
  • Shoulder
  • Back
  • Neck

    • On this central pages, links will be included to pages that are specifically about the types of injuries listed above. This information is not meant to a substitute for medical help, but more as information for proper-self care, the kind of information one would recieve from a personal trainer. Each sub-page will include:
  • Quick description of the type(s) of pain
  • Proper methods for self-care (stretching, icing, compression, etc)
  • Repercussions
  • When to seek medical help
