About

This webpage is a resources for the everyday athlete. This is meant for people who live very active lifestyles but don't have the time or want to be super involved in the fitness space. They just want to be able to maintain their level of activity in a healthy way and play their sport of choice. This is geared mostly towards college students or young professionals that are involved in club teams, intramural sports, and competative circuits, people whoa re very active but do not have the support of a team trainer to help them particpate in their chosen sport in a healthy way.
